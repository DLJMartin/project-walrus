Un cadeau pour Milo - A present for Milo

Spoken Language: English

Target Language: French

Author: Christine Powell

Date Published: 6th May 2024

ISBN: 9789811716867

The whole family thinks that Milo is bored. They have a great idea which might not be to Milo’s liking.

‘Un cadeau pour Milo’ is one of the stories of “La famille Martin” that will help children to learn French at home or at school. ‘Un cadeau pour Milo’ focuses on the topics of family and types of pets. It also introduces the use of the present tense of both verbs Pouvoir et Vouloir in particular.
