Quelle matinee - What a morning!

Spoken Language: English

Target Language: French

Author: Christine Powell

Date Published: 8th May 2024

ISBN: 9789811717109

The children struggle to get ready for school. They don’t yet know that Mum has got plans for them……...

'Quelle matinée!' is one of the stories of 'La famille Martin' that will help children to learn French at home or at school. 'Quelle matinée!' focuses on the topics of daily routine and morning activities. It does so by introducing the use of the present tense of reflexive verbs in particular.
